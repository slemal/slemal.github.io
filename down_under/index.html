I am a PhD student at the Department of Mathematics of the University of Luxembourg, working under the supervision of Bruno Teheux and Nick Bezhanishvili.

I hold a master's degree in Logic from the Institute for Logic, Language and Computation (ILLC) in Amsterdam, as well as a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics from the University of Li√®ge. I wrote my master's thesis under the supervision of Nick Bezhanishvili and Tommaso Moraschini.

My current research focuses on structural completeness, natural dualities and choice free dualities. In my master's thesis I studied the hereditarily structurally complete extensions of wK4. During my PhD, my main goal is to provide choice-free alternatives to 'classical' natural dualities.

My broader research interests include Algebraic Logic, Order Theory, Duality Theory and Universal Algebra.
