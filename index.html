I am a PhD student at the University of Luxembourg, working under the supervision of Bruno Teheux and Nick Bezhanishvili. Before that I did a Bachelor's in Mathematics at the University of Li√®ge, and completed the Master of Logic at the Institute for Logic, Language and Computation (ILLC) in Amsterdam. I wrote my master's thesis under the supervision of Nick Bezhanishvili and Tommaso Moraschini.

My current research focuses on structural completeness, natural dualities and choice free dualities. In my master's thesis I studied the hereditarily structurally complete extensions of wK4. During my PhD, my main goal is to provide choice-free alternatives to 'classical' natural dualities.

My broader research interests include Algebraic Logic, Order Theory, Duality Theory and Universal Algebra.
